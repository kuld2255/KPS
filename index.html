Welcome to KPS Enviro Tech Pvt. Ltd.

Established in 2018, KPS Enviro Tech Pvt. Ltd. is a leading service provider in Integrated Municipal Solid Waste Management (MSWM) and environmental services. With a strong presence across multiple cities and towns, we specialize in door-to-door waste collection, street sweeping, drain desilting, composting, MRF operations, and manpower outsourcing for sanitation and facility management.

Our mission is to drive sustainable waste solutions by integrating technology, community participation, and eco-friendly processes. Whether it's rural or urban infrastructure, we provide end-to-end waste management — from collection and segregation to processing and final disposal — ensuring compliance with Solid Waste Management Rules 2016.

Join us in our vision of a cleaner, greener India through responsible waste handling and public awareness initiatives
